PRELOADER LAB ★ 08 EXPERIMENTS

KM.BBQ
Logo Loaders

Eight loading animations built from the KM.BBQ badge — pure CSS keyframes in a single file, styled to the folk-pop grill-house poster system. Each one loops on its own; click a stage (or hit Replay) to run it again.

01

Sticker Slam

Every piece slaps on like a poster sticker, then the lockup settles.

02

Ember Draw

Chalk-line stroke draw on charcoal, inked in with a warm ember glow.

03

Heat Rise

Color climbs through an ink ghost like heat coming off the grill.

04

Orbit Type

Circular type spins around the breathing badge — the flame keeps flickering.

05

Blinds Counter

Brand-color slats lift one by one while the counter runs to 100.

06

Grate Reveal

The grill grate slides apart over the logo while the smoke keeps rising.

07

Drop & Land

The badge drops in with squash-and-stretch; letters land and the shadow slams on.

08

Sign Flicker

The storefront sign buzzes on at dusk — flame first, letters last.
